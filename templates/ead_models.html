EAD Framework Note
Under Basel III / IFRS 9, EAD represents the expected outstanding balance at the time of default, including undrawn committed amounts drawn down by the borrower as financial difficulties increase.
The CCF model applies a conversion factor to off-balance-sheet commitments. The utilization and Markov models capture the pro-cyclical increase in drawdowns during stress.
